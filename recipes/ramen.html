Fancy (Instant) Ramen

Description

For hangover days. (Enough said :p)

Ingredients

  • Your choice of instant ramen
  • Water
  • Eggs
  • Bok choy
  • Green onions
  • Fish sauce
  • Sesame oil

Steps

  1. Prep all your ingredients! This cooks very quickly, and you don't want your noodles to go soggy.
  2. Add in half of the flavour pack into your pot of water, then boil. Once the water has boil, carefully add in your eggs.
  3. Depending on how cooked you like your eggs, add in your noodles. Continue boiling mixture until noodles are al dente.
  4. Add bok choy, then turn off heat. Let the residual temperature cook the bok choy
  5. Top ramen with green onions, fish sauce, and sesame oil. If the broth is not salty enough, add in more of the flavour pack.
